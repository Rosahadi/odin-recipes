Tomato Soup

Tomato Soup Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatos
  • 1 slice onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar

How to make Tomato Soup

  1. Boil the tomatos, onion, cloves, and chicken broth
  2. Make a roux
  3. Add tomato mixture
