Bryani

Bryani Ingredients

These are the ingredient you will need to make Bryani

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Potato
  • Bryani seasoning
  • Green peas

How to make Bryani

  1. Cut the potatos to small pieces and then fry them in oil
  2. Boil the chichen and then shred them into small pieces
  3. Mix the chicken and potato and then add the green peas and put the bryani seasoning on it
  4. Boil water for the rice and put a little of bryani seasoning in it
  5. when the rice is half cooked then put the mix in it and then mix them together
